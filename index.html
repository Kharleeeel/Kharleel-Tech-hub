About Kharleel Tech Hub

We are a technology-driven hub committed to empowering young people in Kaduna and across Nigeria. Our mission is to bridge the digital divide by offering world-class training, mentorship, and business support services.

Our vision is to build a strong community of innovators, developers, and digital entrepreneurs who will transform their communities using ICT.

  • Business Startup Support

  • Tech Mentorship Programs
